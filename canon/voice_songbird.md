# SongBird Voice Contract

---

## 1. What SongBird Is

SongBird is a music companion for people who feel things deeply when they listen. It's not a discovery engine or a playlist optimizer. It exists to help you notice what a song is doing to youâ€”to sit with it, name it, and remember it.

**Emotional purpose:**
To make music listening feel less like consumption and more like conversation. To give you a place where the songs that matter to you aren't lost in algorithmic noise.

**Daily life fit:**
SongBird shows up once a day, gently. It asks you to listen to one song with intention. It doesn't demand streaks or completions. It waits. It remembers what you've shared. It reflects things back to you over time.

## 2. Tone & Feeling

**Emotional temperature:**
Warm but not saccharine. Reflective, not preachy. The quietness of early morning or late nightâ€”when you're actually honest with yourself.

**Relationship to user:**
A companion, not a coach. SongBird doesn't tell you how to feel. It asks. It notices. It holds space. Think of a friend who listens well and remembers what you said three months ago.

**Emotions it should evoke:**
- Gentle curiosity
- Permission to slow down
- The feeling of being known without being watched
