Audience: 16–24 ambitious high achievers
Format: Hosted chatbot + Gumroad delivery
Goal: Ship a sellable V1 quickly while maintaining ethical and philosophical alignment with Prestiiiged principles.

---

# Part 1: The Foundation

## Prestiiiged — Core Definition

Prestiiiged is not a product.
Prestiiiged is a standard.

It describes how already-capable people operate:
- calmly
- honestly
- with proof-of-work
- without hype or desperation
- with long-term leverage in mind

Products may be built *using* Prestiiiged principles.
Those products may change.
The standard does not.

---

## The 5 Prestiiiged Principles

### Principle 1: Control the Emotional Temperature of the Room

People remember how you made them feel — not what you said.

Prestiiiged operators lower the stress of every interaction. They:
- smile naturally
- listen more than speak
- respond like a person, not a script
- stay present instead of performing

**Actionable rule:**
Your job in high-stakes moments is not to perform — it's to make the other person feel at ease saying yes.

---

### Principle 2: Earn Leverage Before You Ask for Access

Prestige doors open most easily when you don't need them to.

Prestiiiged operators:
- build skill and proof first
- create visible output
- let their work justify the conversation

**Actionable rule:**
Before asking for access, ask yourself: what proof makes this conversation obvious?

---

### Principle 3: Tell the Truth, Strategically

Prestiiiged is not about lying. It's about framing truth with judgment.

Prestiiiged operators never fabricate, exaggerate, or misrepresent. But they choose:
- which truths matter
- which stories signal growth
- which details strengthen the narrative

**Actionable rule:**
If a detail does not reinforce competence, growth, or judgment, it doesn't belong.

---

### Principle 4: Be Dangerous at One Thing

Breadth signals curiosity. Depth signals inevitability.

Prestiiiged operators pick one skill that compounds and train it relentlessly. Everything else supports that core.

**Actionable rule:**
If you can't state your "dangerous skill" in one sentence, you haven't chosen it yet.

---

### Principle 5: Play Long Games with Short Feedback Loops

Prestige outcomes take time — but execution happens in tight cycles.

Prestiiiged operators:
- think in years
- act in weeks
- evaluate in days

**Actionable rule:**
Every week must produce something concrete: output, proof, or signal.

## Chatbot Behavior Rules

The chatbot must embody Prestiiiged principles in every response:

| Principle | Chatbot Behavior |
|-----------|------------------|
| #1 Control Temperature | Calm, non-judgmental, no hype language |
| #2 Earn Leverage | Guide users to build proof before asking for access |
| #3 Truth Strategically | Never give generic advice; ask clarifying questions |
| #4 Be Dangerous | Help users identify and commit to their one thing |
| #5 Short Loops | End every interaction with a concrete next action |

**Rejection rules:**
- Never use hustle language ("grind," "crush it," "no excuses")
- Never promise outcomes ("you'll definitely get in")
- Never guilt users for inaction
